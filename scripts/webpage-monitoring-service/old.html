Housing

The online housing tool will open in mid-August. Hotel information, including nightly rates and proximity to the convention center, are listed below. See city map for details.

Only single and double room rates are listed. Most hotels offer triple and quadruple rates, which will be available via the online reservation process. U.S. Government Per Diem rates require a valid U.S. Government Identification,  are limited in availability, not offered at every hotel, and available on a first-come, first-served basis.
